Elvis's Story

Elvis is the beloved Shetland pony companion of the RonyaSane stable. Small in size but enormous in personality, Elvis brings joy and smiles to everyone who meets him.

He loves nothing more than a gentle trail ride through the Kopparnäs forest paths, and he has a special talent for introducing the very youngest riders to the magical world of horses.

Elvis is the perfect first pony — patient, gentle and endlessly entertaining. A true character and the favourite of every visitor to the yard.
