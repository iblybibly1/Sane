About Ronya

Vähän minusta

Hi, I'm Ronya!

Born in 1998 and based in Inkoo, Finland, I've been working with horses since I was 2 years old. That means over 20 years of daily life with these magnificent animals — and I still love every single moment.

I started competing at age 4 in 2002 and have competed at regional levels in jumping, dressage and cross-country events over the years. I've trained under several accomplished instructors who have shaped my approach and philosophy.

My greatest achievement isn't a trophy or ribbon — it's the feeling when a horse learns something new. That moment of connection and understanding between horse and human is what drives me every day.

I operate from my yard near the beautiful Kopparnäs recreational area. The facilities include a lit riding arena, gravel paths and scenic forest trails — perfect for all disciplines.
