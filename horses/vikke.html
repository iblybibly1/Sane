Vikke's Story

Vikke is a beautiful stallion who had a remarkable career in dressage and in-hand showing under Ronya's training and care. Elegant, powerful and full of presence, Vikke always caught the eye in the arena.

He excelled in in-hand showing, demonstrating the natural grace and movement that made him stand out from the crowd. His dressage work was equally impressive — precise, expressive and a joy to watch.

After a wonderful career, Vikke retired in 2024. He now enjoys a peaceful and well-deserved retirement at the yard, loved by everyone who visits.
