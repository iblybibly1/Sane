Beso's Story

Beso arrived from Spain in 2021 as a young, promising horse full of talent and curiosity. From day one it was clear he had something special — natural athleticism, a brave attitude and a wonderful character.

Under Ronya's careful and patient training, Beso has developed beautifully. He made his competition debut in Helsinki in 2023 and has been growing in confidence and skill ever since.

Beso is our main competition prospect — a horse with a very exciting future ahead in jumping. We can't wait to see what he will achieve.
