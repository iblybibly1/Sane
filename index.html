Hi! I'm Ronya, born 1998 and based in Inkoo, Finland. I've been working with horses since I was 2 years old and competing since 2002 — over 20 years of passion, learning and growth.

I offer riding lessons, training, horse sitting and event services from my yard near the beautiful Kopparnäs recreational area. The facilities include a lit riding arena, gravel paths and forest trails.

I've competed in jumping, dressage and cross-country at regional levels and trained under several accomplished instructors. My greatest achievement? The feeling when a horse learns something new.
