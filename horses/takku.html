Takku's Story

Takku is one of the most versatile and reliable horses at RonyaSane. With a calm temperament and willingness to work, Takku has become a favourite among riders of all levels.

Whether it's a beginner finding their seat for the first time or an experienced rider working on technical exercises, Takku adapts beautifully and gives 100% every time.

Takku excels in both arena work and trail riding along the beautiful paths near Kopparnäs. A true all-rounder and the heart of the stable.
